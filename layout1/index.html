Building Surveys

We undertake survey of domestic and commercial properties. Reports are based on a visual inspection, no opening up takes place however the property is checked for any structural defects i.e. subsidence, dry rot, wet rot, foundation failure and pyrite. Services are checked. Electrics and heating systems are not tested.

Snag Lists (new builds)

Snag Lists are carried out on new build properties. Snag Lists cover minor defective or incomplete items and workmanship. Snag Lists are emailed to Builder within 24 hours of inspection.

Land Registry Compliant Maps.

Land Registry Compliant Maps for first registration. Floor plans are required for apartments for first registration. Boundary maps prepared. Boundary maps prepared in the event of disputes. Party wall agreements are usually prepared when adjoining properties agree to share a boundary.
